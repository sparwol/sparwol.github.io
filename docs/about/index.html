About

In an increasingly digital world, the security of your systems, data, and operations isn’t just a concern—it’s a business imperative. Cybersecurity isn't merely about "firewalls" and "antivirus software." It's about protecting your reputation, your intellectual property, your clients, and your future.

My name is Sean, and I specialize in fortifying the foundations of businesses through a holistic, strategic approach to cybersecurity. Think of me as your personal cyber bodyguard, with the skills, tools, and knowledge to ward off the digital wolves at the gate.

My Background

    • Sean Parrott-Wolfe

    • Information Security Consultant

    I’m Sean, the founder of Sparwol Security, and I’ve been working in IT and cybersecurity for over 15 years. My career has given me the opportunity to explore both offensive and defensive security, focusing on areas like healthcare, industrial environments, and small-to-medium businesses (SMBs).

    I’m passionate about understanding how systems work—whether it’s securing a network, finding vulnerabilities, or helping organizations build stronger defenses. My experience spans Windows environments, Active Directory, cryptography, open-source intelligence, and physical security, but I’m always eager to learn more and expand my perspective.

    One of my goals is to make cybersecurity accessible for businesses of all sizes, particularly SMBs that might not have the resources for large-scale security teams. I believe that practical, thoughtful solutions can go a long way in creating a safer, more resilient environment for everyone.

    When I’m not working on security projects, I spend time exploring new tools and techniques, connecting with the broader cybersecurity community, and continuously developing my skills. I value curiosity, collaboration, and the opportunity to make a positive impact.

    Certifications: A+, Net+, Sec+, MCSA, MCSE, PNPT, CISSP
