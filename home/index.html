About

Background

Background

Background

I began altering AUTOEXEC.BAT and CONFIG.SYS files thirty years ago. Since then, I have focused primarily on Windows networks, Active Directory, and applied cryptography. I have worked on a larger team supporting large scale networking and security needs for an international financial institution comprised of mainframes, databases, servers, and over 40,000 endpoint users. I have worked on a team of less than 10 supporting an educational institution involving a wide variety of servers, over 200 lab and classroom workstations, and over 6000 bring-your-own-device endpoints. I have also worked on a very personal level, providing one-on-one support to individual clients. In any instance, I believe in a whole-picture view of a client's needs, which assets are most important to protect, and how secure is the weakest link.
