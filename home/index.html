Sean Parrott-Wolfe (AKA sparwol) is a private information security and cybersecurity consultant offering a variety of services. 


In order to effectively address a client's security needs, a whole-picture view is required. Which assets are most valuable or most important to protect? Where is the weakest link and how secure is it? How well are employees trained and educated? These are critical questions that must be asked. 


I began altering AUTOEXEC.BAT and CONFIG.SYS files in MSDOS and writing programs in QBasic thirty years ago. The web was still in its infancy. Since then, I have strived to learn and continually develop new skills within the security realm. I have primarily focused on Windows networks and Active Directory, applied cryptography and cryptanalysis, open-source intelligence, and physical security. 


My recent learning interests are in red team operations, industrial IoT, and mobile applications.
